Vision, Mission & Core Values

Our guiding principles for excellence

Our Vision

Quality and relevant education for its clientele to be globally competitive, culture-sensitive, and morally responsive human resources for sustainable development.

Our Mission

Help accelerate socio-economic development, promote harmony among diverse communities, and improve quality of life through instruction, research, extension, and resource generation in Southern Philippines.

Core Values

GREAT USM

We commit ourselves to uphold the highest standards of excellence, integrity, and service in all areas of learning, research, and community engagement.

GOODNESS

We practice honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in our decisions and actions, guided by moral values and social responsibility.

RESPONSIVENESS

We actively listen and respond to the needs of students, stakeholders, and communities with respect, inclusivity, and compassion.

EXCELLENCE

We continuously strive for quality and innovation in teaching, learning, research, and service to achieve outstanding performance and outcomes.

ASSERTION of RIGHT

We uphold justice, fairness, and respect for human rights while fostering teamwork, participation, and shared responsibility within the community.

TRUTH

We value truthfulness, accountability, and integrity in personal conduct, academic work, and environmental stewardship.
