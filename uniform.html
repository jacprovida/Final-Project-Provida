Our uniform policy promotes equality, school pride, and a focused learning environment. All students are expected to wear the prescribed uniform properly and with pride.

Daily Uniform - Male

Upper Body

  • White polo shirt with School Seal
  • School ID with Lanyard

Lower Body

  • Black Slacks
  • Black Leather Shoes with Black Socks

Daily Uniform - Female

Upper Body

  • White Blouse
  • Green Necktie
    with Printed Collage and Program
  • School ID with Lanyard

Lower Body

  • School Skirt
  • Black Closed Shoes

Daily Uniform - Muslim Female

Upper Body

  • White Hijab
  • Long-Sleeved White Blouse
  • Green Necktie
    with Printed Collage and Program
  • School ID with Lanyard

Lower Body

  • School Long Skirt
  • Black Closed Shoes

Physical Education Uniform

To be worn on scheduled P.E. days:

  • School P.E. shirt (green with orange trim)
  • Green athletic shorts or track pants
  • White athletic socks and athletic shoes

Formal/Special Occasions Uniform

For assemblies, ceremonies, field trips, and special events:

Boys

  • White long-sleeve dress shirt
  • Green necktie with orange stripes
  • Green dress pants
  • Black leather dress shoes
  • Black belt

Girls

  • White long-sleeve blouse
  • Green ribbon/bow tie
  • Green formal skirt or dress pants
  • Black leather dress shoes
  • White stockings or tights (with skirt)

Grooming Standards

Hair

  • Must be neat, clean, and natural color
  • Boys: Above collar, off ears, above eyebrows
  • Girls: Long hair must be tied back
  • Hair accessories in school colors only

General

  • Nails must be clean and trimmed
  • No visible tattoos or body piercings (except earrings for girls)
  • Minimal makeup for senior students only
  • No extreme hairstyles or colors

Uniform Violations

Consequences for non-compliance:

  • First offense: Verbal warning and parent notification
  • Second offense: Written warning and parent meeting
  • Third offense: Detention and mandatory parent conference
  • Continued violations: Suspension until compliance is met

Students who arrive without proper uniform may be required to wear temporary school-issued clothing or may be sent home to change.
